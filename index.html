About Miami Home Loans

Founded in 2008 during the housing market crisis, Miami Home Loans emerged with a mission to provide transparent, ethical mortgage solutions to South Florida residents. Our founder, having witnessed the impact of predatory lending practices, established MHL on the principles of integrity, education, and client empowerment.

Today, we remain committed to our core values of transparency, excellence, and community service. Our team of certified mortgage professionals works tirelessly to secure the best possible terms for our clients while providing comprehensive education throughout the lending process. Frequently Asked Questions

What credit score do I need to qualify for a mortgage?

While requirements vary by loan type, we work with scores as low as 580 for FHA loans and 620 for conventional mortgages. We'll help you understand your options regardless of your credit situation.

How much down payment will I need?

Down payments can be as low as 3.5% for FHA loans or 3% for conventional loans. VA loans may require no down payment. We'll help you explore all available options and down payment assistance programs.

How long does the entire mortgage process take?

Most loans close within 30 days, though we can expedite to as little as 14 days when needed. Pre-approval typically takes 24 hours or less with complete documentation.

What documents will I need to provide?

Generally, you'll need recent pay stubs, W-2s, tax returns, bank statements, and ID. Self-employed borrowers may need additional documentation. We'll provide a detailed checklist based on your situation.

Are there any fees for getting pre-approved?

No, we offer complimentary pre-approvals with no obligation. You'll only incur fees if you decide to proceed with a full mortgage application.
